# Product Definition

UGCLabs is not a video editor.

UGCLabs is a short-form affiliate creative production tool that turns one reference video into multiple upload-ready TikTok/Reels creatives.

## One-Line Pitch

UGCLabs helps affiliate marketers generate 10-30 short-form ad variants from one reference video in minutes.

## Target Users

- TikTok Shop affiliate marketers
- Instagram/Reels affiliate creators
- Dropshipping marketers
- App and SaaS affiliate promoters
- Short-form creator operators managing many accounts
- Small agencies distributing video packs to creators

## Core Job

The user provides:

- Reference video
- Product or app description
- Key selling points
- Desired format
- Number of variants

UGCLabs returns:

- 0-30 second vertical MP4 videos
- ZIP download
- Individual MP4 files
- Captions and hashtags for higher plans
- Creator delivery links for higher plans

## Product Principle

Speed and volume matter more than perfect single-video craft.

The product should help users create enough decent creatives quickly so they can test what works across TikTok/Reels accounts.

## External Positioning

Use:

- Affiliate creative productivity
- Short-form creative variant generation
- UGC-style video pack generation
- Upload-ready TikTok/Reels creatives

Avoid:

- Spam evasion
- Shadowban prevention guarantees
- Hash laundering
- Platform policy bypass
- Automated mass posting
